Youth Entrepreneurs Day

NU E-Club Academy

September 13, 2023

NU E-Club is having its mega event focused on entrepreneurship, scheduled for the 23rd of February 2024. This event aims to bring together the entrepreneurial community, students, and enthusiasts to celebrate and learn from the journeys of successful startups, engage in insightful talks, and enjoy an entertaining session to end the day on a high note.

Why attend Youth Entrepreneurs Day?:

  1. Learn from the journeys of successful startups
  2. Engage in insightful talks led by industry experts
  3. Connect with like-minded individuals and build valuable networks
  4. Enjoy an entertaining session to wrap up the day on a high note

Whether you're a high school student, a university student, or a fresh graduate interested in entrepreneurship, then this event is made for you! Don't miss out on this incredible opportunity to enhance your skills, gain valuable insights, and be part of a thriving entrepreneurial community.
